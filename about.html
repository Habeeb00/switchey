Details

This extension is designed to enhance your Google account experience by providing additional features and tools to improve productivity and usability.

How to Use

Set Default Account

1. Click the extension icon in your toolbar
2. Select your preferred account from the dropdown
3. Click "Set as Default" to make it your primary account

Quick Account Switch

Use the dropdown menu to easily switch between your accounts without logging out and back in

Gmail Integration

Your selected default account will automatically be used when opening Gmail in new tabs

Temporary Disable

Use the "Disable Extension" toggle in the extension popup when you need to temporarily bypass the default account behavior

Theme Preference

Click the theme toggle button (top-right corner) to switch between light and dark modes for comfortable viewing

Instant Effect

All changes take effect immediately for new tabs - no need to refresh or restart your browser
