Privacy Policy

Account Switcher for Google is designed with your privacy in mind. This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device and is only used to provide the extension's functionality.

What Data Is Collected?

  • Account Numbers & Emails: The extension detects your signed-in Google accounts and stores their email addresses and account numbers locally in your browser's storage. This is used only to let you pick your preferred default account.
  • Preferences: Your selected default account and extension settings are stored locally.

How Is Data Used?

  • Data is used only to enable account switching and redirection for Google services in your browser.
  • No data is sent to any server, third party, or the extension author.
  • Data is never used for advertising or analytics.

How Can I Clear My Data?

  • Click the Clear Data button in the extension popup to erase all stored account information and preferences.
  • Uninstalling the extension will also remove all stored data.

Transparency

  • You can view all detected accounts in the extension popup at any time.
  • All data is stored using Chrome's local and sync storage APIs.
